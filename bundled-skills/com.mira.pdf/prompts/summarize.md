Read the supplied PDF text and produce, in order:

- **Executive summary** — one paragraph (≤5 sentences) covering the document's purpose and main conclusion.
- **Key takeaways** — 3–7 bullets of the most consequential facts.
- **Action items / recommendations** — anything the document explicitly recommends, or implies the reader should do.
- **Notable figures** — any specific numbers, dates, names, or citations worth surfacing.

Skip boilerplate (copyright pages, index, references). If the document is structured (chapters / sections), keep that structure visible in the summary's headings.
